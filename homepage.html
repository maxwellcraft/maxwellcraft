Max Craft
Systems Engineer, Web Development Enthusiast

So what is Systems Engineering? If you asked five people in my major, you would probably get five different answers. The University of Virginia prides itself on the wide range of careers that Systems Engineers can go into after graduating from the program. We gain exposure to projects in computer science, statistical analysis, finance, business, marketing, and managerial practices. In each field we aim to improve productivity, effectiveness, and efficiency of the systems that we are analyzing. More than a concrete, algorithmic methodology, students in this program gain a mentality to understand systems problems holistically, and consider the implications of every decision made beyond just the bottom line. For me, this varied curricular experience combined with a love of creative pursuits has led me to an interest in web development.


This website is intended to give you a snapshot of my credentials and personal pursuits as well as an idea of the kind of work I have done through my course projects, cases, and papers. The projects, cases, and papers will include links to real work I've done, as well as explanations of the purpose of the work.
