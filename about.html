About Maxwell Craft Designs

Maxwell Craft Designs is a little bit of everything. An online platform for showcasing the products I've designed and built, the food that I've made and recipes I've modified, and the thoughts I think people could benefit from reading. (Hopefully) valuable nuggets of information side-by-side with guides to woodworking projects, kitchen experiments, and freelance capabilities.

Products

Woodcraft is my section for the woodworking products that I've created. Primarily furniture with a portfolio of home gifts and bentwood rings as well.

Foodcraft is multipurpose, with an assortment of food and cooking products, as well as a collection of recipes, food reviews and discussions

Passions

Lifecraft is a chronicle of my travels and adventures, life hacks, and general musings that I want to share

Wealthcraft is my attempt to help folks with their questions and uncertanties around personal finance. I think most educational systems, at least in the U.S., have done students a great disservice with financial education and I hope the content here can help people understand their financial situations.
